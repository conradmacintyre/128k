About DGR Bingo

Hi. My name is Conrad. I'm a web developer. I made this because my wife and I enjoyed quoting Dave (oh, so quotable) and laughing at his catchphrases and I decided to make a game of it. We have been watching DGR videos competitively ever since. haha. I hope you also enjoy it. If you have suggestions for ways to improve DGR Bingo, send me a tweet at @conradmacintyre.

I am not affiliated with Dave or his channel in any official way. I don't make any money from this, and other than Google Anaylitics I don't collect any data.
